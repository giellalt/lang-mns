List of postpositions based on Kálmán 1976, Riese 2001 and Ромбандеева 2017.
Paradigms of postposition+Px constructions based on corpus data.
The -па̄л paradigm stands for all the compund forms ending in па̄л, written with or without a hyphen.
There may be further postpositions taking Pxs that were not to be found in the corpus.
