я̄тем: я̄+diminutive suffix+PxSg1
нэ̄квен: нэ̄+diminutive suffix+PxSg2
ӯскве: ӯс+diminutive suffix
а̄щкем: а̄щ+diminutive suffix+PxSg1

Diminutive suffix forms: кве, ке, те
It comes right after the stem, it is followed by personal suffixes, than case endings

о̄тырен, о̄тыре: it is not a syncopating stem
а̄тпаг: that is the translative form of the adjective а̄туп, which is derived from the noun а̄т 'hair'
областьт: locative of область
